雅思口语补完计划

Feb 1, 2019 | 技术

本文算是我为了拯救我屎一般的雅思口语（还有写作）而做的一点准备笔记。

雅思口语因为其形式限制，能力不足的情况下是一定要做提前准备的。就像网上考试大神们说的那样，一定要整理题型，但是我非常讨厌整理也非常讨厌做笔记也非常讨厌虚伪的背答案，所以暂时只用这种粗暴的方式：把雅思哥的题目过一遍，自己一题题写答案，提前熟悉，然后看造化。我一岁零七个月才会说话，小学前不敢自己买东西，初中前说话快了会打结，所以我深信对我来说说谎要消耗更多的大脑计算力，Telling the truth 不仅使得出现意外时的思路有迹可循，也可以把对话向深刻的方向引导。

其实记在纸上一般是最好的办法，但是接下来十一天我不在国内，而且我特别讨厌阅读手写的笔记（特别是我自己的）

  • What work do you do?
    I have no job now. I just graduated from my university last year and I am preparing to study/pursue a master degree abroad in September this year.

  • Why did you choose to do that type of work (or that job)?
    Because I need some time for preparations, such as IELTS. If things go well, I want to do an internship before I leave. (pre-sessional course)

  • Do you miss being a student?
    To some extent, Yes. When I was a student, I had no need to worry about money and social relations. But, as a student, there were too many disciplines which made me feel that I was confined.

  • Why did you choose to study that subject?
    Well, I didn’t mean to choose Computer Science. I was transferred to this subject because I failed to make enough grades for Economy at Gaokao. But I found it turned out to be quite interesting. I like its certainty and I have to say the IT industry is profitable.

  • What subject are you studying?
    I majored in Computer Science. In this subject, we studyed every aspect of the computer. From the lower level, such as the CPU and the circuitry, to the middle level including Operation systems and Computer Network, finally ended in the higher level which includes software development and programming languages. By the way, there was plenty of math.

  • Do you prefer to study in the mornings or in the afternoons?
    I’d like to go with studying in the afternoons. The reason is simple. Because I hate getting up early. If I did so, I might feel dizzy and might find it difficult to concentrate. So I have no choice.

  • Are you looking forward to working?
    No, I am not. I think I’m not fully prepared on my expertize and I still need time to deepen my knowledge. This is the reason I choose to pursue a master degree.

  • Do you like your subject?
    Yes, I do. At first, I thought programming was too sophisticated to be mastered. But then I found it quite attractive provided that we were patient and hard-working. Programming is a kind like playing God since we can indicate and determine everything in our program. A harmoniously running program can make us feel satisfied.
